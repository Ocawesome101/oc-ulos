NAME
  sh - bourne-ish shell

SYNOPSIS
  sh [-e]

DESCRIPTION
  sh is a Bourne-shell clone.  It is deprecated in favor of lsh(1).

  There is half-implemented scripting and piping support.  sh is unsupported and may be removed in a future release.
